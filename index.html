// what's in the cup

We're a little
obsessive about it.

Whole fruit, never concentrate

We blend the actual fruit — skin, fibre and all where it counts. Nothing comes out of a carton labelled "from".

Zero added sugar

The sweetness is the fruit doing its job. If a blend needs a lift, it's a touch of raw honey or dates — that's the whole list.

Pressed every morning

Produce arrives at 6am, gets pressed and blended on site, and we toss whatever doesn't sell. Tomorrow we start over.
